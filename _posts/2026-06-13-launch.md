Welcome to the Somalia Security Monitor — a data-driven platform tracking conflict dynamics across Somalia and the Horn of Africa.

This site publishes daily situation reports, monthly trend analyses, and in-depth analytical pieces, all grounded in a live dataset of verified security incidents sourced from UCDP, live RSS monitoring, and GDELT — updated daily.

## What to expect

**Daily situation reports** covering the most significant security developments across Somalia's Federal Member States — Benadir, Southwest, Hirshabelle, Jubaland, Galmudug, Puntland, and Somaliland.

**Monthly trend analyses** examining patterns in incident frequency, fatality rates, actor activity, and geographic spread.

**Occasional deep-dives** on specific dynamics — Al-Shabaab operational patterns, civilian targeting trends, the role of external actors, and more.

**An interactive dashboard** launching soon, with live maps and charts that update automatically every day from the underlying incident database.

The analysis here prioritizes verifiable data over speculation. Every claim is traceable to the incident record.

More coming soon.
