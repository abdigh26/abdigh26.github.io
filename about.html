About

Somali OSINT Brief

Somali OSINT Brief is a Somalia security analysis platform run by Professionals, focused on conflict dynamics, insurgency patterns, and geopolitical developments across the Horn of Africa. The work sits at the intersection of ground-level incident data and strategic analysis.

This site publishes weekly situation reports, monthly trend analyses, and occasional deep-dives — all grounded in a live dataset of 50k+ verified security incidents sourced from ACLED and updated weekly.

The Data

The underlying dataset tracks security incidents across Somalia from January 1997 to present, covering incident type, actors, locations, fatalities, and more. It is updated automatically every Monday from ACLED's conflict database.

The Approach

Ground-level data meets grand strategy. Every analytical claim is traceable to verified incident records. No vague assessments, no speculation presented as fact.
